UCLEM launching and Commissioning

On November 3, 2024, at 9:30 a.m. – 10:30 a.m. on SU Church the University Christian Life and Emphasis Month (UCLEM) launching and commissioning was happening. Headed by Rev. Jonathan R. Pia the launching was a success and a start for a new Christian month commenced and I am looking forward to the activities that UCLEM has to offer. I was a spectator in this and I witnessed the official launching.

During the mass, I learned the significance of UCLEM for Sillimanians to enhance the spiritual values of us students and to enhance their education about religion. My initial expectation for the mass was to have supporter who are willing to give their time and energy to serve for this year’s UCLEM and my expectations was satisfied. With the theme “Faith Over Fear: Facing life’s Challenges” made an impact on my self since there are lots of challenges as a student. I don’t have any specific person who made an impression on me but the number of individuals who is willing to serve and in order for this event to be successful.

As I continue to the mass it made me wonder if maybe in the future I can become like those people and it really made a shift in my personal perspective as what are those people’s stories for them to willingly serve. The SU Church is a significant part of Silliman’s culture and Christian tradition so I will expect more in the future to join their events. The activity is beneficial for those students who have gone astray to the path of God and this UCLEM will help them go back due to the vast activities the month of November has to offer.

Because of this activity I have become more curious to what the month of November has to offer due to the vast number of activities it has. Also, I become aware of the number of individuals who are willing to serve.

UCLEM Youth Worship

On November 3, 2024, at 5:30 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. the UCLEM Youth Worshiping was taking place at the SU Church. Headed by Rev. Jonathtan R. Cal, individuals from Silliman gathered at SU Church on this day to take part of the Worship and that includes me. Its main objective was to strengthen the spiritual bond of individuals using the theme “Faith Over Fear: Facing life’s Giants”.

The activity made me realize the importance of spiritual courage and I learned how to battle my inner demons thanks to the theme provided. Since it was said to be a worship, I expected it to be more on singing talented singers using their skills to use it for good. I was not disappointed. It was an activity where I witnessed the full cooperation of individuals in the mass singing in unison together with the leader. Also, in this activity I was able to witness individuals facing their own giants through prayer.

The session changed my perspective about how to tackle challenges, it can be through a prayer or singing to God and offering myself to him and it’s up to me if I take action or not to face the challenges. I learned in this event that faith in God alone won’t fix your personal problems since you also need to take action.

Because of this activity my belief was broadened and I was able to see other people that are going through stuff gather and face their own “Giant’s”. I should take note that faith will not fix my problems since I realized that God also wants me to deal with it.

1st Misa de Aguinaldo

On December 16, 2024 at 4:30 a.m. to 6:00 a.m. I attending the the 1st Misa de Aquinaldo which was held in Dumaguete City. The main goal of the 1st Misa De Aquinaldo is to prepare for Christmas by attending the nine-day novena until reaching the birth of Jesus Christ.

In the event I have learned the importance of this mass and it is more about my spiritual reflections like what is the meaning of this mass and as to why we do it. I anticipated that the atmosphere of the cathedral would be more spiritual and I can attest to this since even if I am outside, I can still feel the ambiance of the mass resonating withing and out of the cathedral. The whole experience made my spiritual understanding on the mass deeper and even if I am outside with no chairs I did not consider it as a negative sign since I don’t want to be pessimistic Infront of the lord.

Even though it is early in the morning there were plenty of people gathering for a single mass causing the inner parts of the church to be quickly populated. With this information I realized there are plenty of people who would get up in the morning and give their time and effort to the lord and I can say from there actions that individuals who gathered there really have a deep faith and I am inspired by this.

Because of this activity I become gained knowledge as to why people are performing this task and I also learned that there are plenty of people would give their time and energy to attend a mass so early in the morning.
